Jollof Rice

Made from rice, tomatoes, peppers, onions, and other seasonings, Jollof Rice is a staple in Nigerian cuisine.

2 hr, Yields 1 pot

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Canola Oil
  • 6 Medium Tomatoes
  • 6 Red Poblano Peppers
  • 3 Medium Red Onions
  • 1 Hot Pepper
  • 3 tbps of Tomato Paste
  • Curry Powder, Thyme, Bay Leaves, Garlic Powder, Salt, Pepper, to Taste
  • 3 tsps of Maggi
  • 5 Cups Water
  • 4 Cups of Rice

Instructions

  1. Par boil your rice (i.e. do not fully cook your rice).
  2. In a food processor, combine tomatoes, poblano peppers, chopped onions, and hot pepper with 2 cups of stock.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil and stir fry sliced onions.
  4. Add your seasonings and tomato pastek stir frying for 5 minutes. Reduce heat of mixture, and set at medium heat for 10 to 12 minutes
  5. Add 4 cups of the stock to the tomato sauce and bring to a boil for 1-2 minutes
  6. Add the rice to the pot, cooking through the rest of the rice within the mixture for about 30 minutes. Let rest.
Yummy! Enjoy!
