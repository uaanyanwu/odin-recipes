Puff Puff

Puff Puff is a delicious fried dough snack, commmonly eaten in many African countries (often known by different names!)

1 hr 20 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 1/4 cups Water
  • 2 tsps Yeast
  • 1 tsp Nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • Oil for Deep Frying

Instructions

  1. Mix half a cup of warm water, 1 tsp of sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Cover with a lid and set aside for about 4-6 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, and nutmeg)
  3. Pour proofed yeast and one cup of water with the dry ingredients, mix until batter is lump free.
  4. Cover dough and allow to rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat oil in a pan deep and wide enough to 360 F.
  6. Scoop 1/4 tbsp of dough and drop into the oil
  7. Fry both sides until golden brown.
  8. Drain on a paper towel. Allow to rest.
Yummy! Enjoy!
